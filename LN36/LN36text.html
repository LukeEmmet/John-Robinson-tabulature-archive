Music supplement to Lute News 36 (December 1995): In dulci jubilo

‘In dulci jubilo, &c’ Esaias Reusner [the elder], Musicalischer Lustgarten, Breslau 1645, p. [3] - copy in Gdansk. Thanks to Dick Hoban for bringing it to my attention.

An offering for the festive season - Reusner’s arrangement of this still popular tune comes from his collection of complete solo lute psalm settings.
